> # flânerie  
>
> As someone who can barely read and write English, I much enjoy the vibrations of linguistic archives, which are my only means to flânerie: with water packed and food cast, I travel alongside Walter and marvel at the strokes of grained language.*  
> 
> Yet, being England-born with roots grown from ‘rivers of blood,’ I move counterclockwise from a land that wishes to see me bleed: red or blue?
