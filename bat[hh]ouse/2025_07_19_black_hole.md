# Black Hole

I can draw a **line**,    
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;and, you can draw a line,    
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;and, they can draw a line,    
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;and, all can draw a line.   

And, **perhaps**,    
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;it is somewhere, among,    
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;all these lines,    
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;in which `polynomials` form?    

And, further in,    
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;**perhaps**:    
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;it is within such lines that observation is homed.    

But, you see, stars, too, can draw lines.    
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And, when they **die**?    
They `eat themselves whole`.
