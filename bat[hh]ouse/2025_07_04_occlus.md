# Occlus

*i was caught.*   

*Hook, line **and** breaths away,*   
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*from a family meeting.*   

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Yes, i’ll pass your greeting.   

*Yet . .*   
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*my gulps remained steady as i swam,*    
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*—hooked **and** lined.*    

*When i looked behind,*   
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*you’d gone to the meeting,*   
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*without my greeting.*
