# Occlus



i was caught. 

Hook, line and breaths away from a family meeting. 

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*Yes, i’ll pass your greeting.* 

Yet, my gulps remained steady as i swam, , hooked and lined. 

When i looked behind, you’d gone to the meeting, without my greeting.
