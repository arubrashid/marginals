# Splut Drums



Must you splutter your coughing? 

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That weeping drum,   

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*the one over there,*   
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*yes, there,*   

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;is now spluttering too—   


>*:  2025.07.14, ‘splut drums’, written by the neighbour of a coughing man 
(—neighbour being me; arub (rashid))*
