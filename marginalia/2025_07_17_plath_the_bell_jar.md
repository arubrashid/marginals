# The Bell Jar

**For, I am no longer a teen, and I never sat in a cafeteria, yogurt in hand; as I, to some despair, am British. And so, I held my cutlery, in hand: wrong and despairingly (—lectures to come); yet, today, on a day that is not auspicious, I have unwittingly decided to converse, to some marginal degree, with The Bell Jar.** 

**And, so—so, let us proceed with despair.**
