# Lolita  
### by Vladimir Nabokov 

**This is a (singular—much ado) thought I hold on the book, ‘Lolita’, which I have not graced myself to read; while I am not discussing the text, I am however, be it in clear vision and hearing, writing in response to its peculiar reception.**

*You see that chair?*

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*small and pretty.*  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*dolled and funny.*  

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;*Don’t treat it like your sugar rack, it’ll make your cavities crack.*
