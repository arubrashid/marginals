# Neologistic Punch

Narration; the type one may encounter ‘*here*’ *(—‘here’ being a position relative to one’s seat at the table)*, is intentionally held in ambiguity. 

Such ambiguity is set to serve the **recursive fracticality of cognitive thought**.

>*Wherein:*   

>*fracticality, much like fractals, reflects self-similarity but not strict self-identity: composition persists across recursion, yet arrangement, relation, or form flexes in deformation.*   

>*This distinction departs from popular interpretations, especially within physics: which often conflate self-similarity with static replication, overlooking the role of emergent asymmetry or relational variance.*   

And so, the narratorial position of ‘*here*’ is akin to a **cognitive swallow**, where *sustenance comes in the form of concurrent, cyclical, and deformative selves*: ‘*here*’, swallows are never static: in thought, or, in form.

>*Wherein:*   

>*deformative is accepted in a thermodynamic sense: implying transformation under strain.*    

>*—it does not necessarily bear negative connotations.*   

In an effort to feed one’s mind, one may find themselves at a ‘[*neologistic punch*](./2025_07_22_calcium.md)’, where *sustenance becomes reliant on invention*.

Or, perhaps, in academic estimate, one may put forth: in conditions where epistemic recursion resists static linguistic forms, **neologism functions not ornamentally but metabolically**. It is not merely a linguistic innovation, but a thermodynamic necessity of cognition under strain.
