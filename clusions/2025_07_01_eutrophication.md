# Eutrophication

Let us begin by hanging our hats, and situating ourselves to the side of the **camel** *(—the camel of objectivity, that is)*, and let us not wonder the absence of the **sheep** *(—the sheep of subjectivity, that is)*, for such [lamenting](../bat[hh]ouse/2025_07_01_filial_piety.md) is not for *‘now’*. Rather, we must map the terrain of *‘before’*.

The terrain *‘before’* is rocky and old in debate, that of: *camel and sheep*, or more epistemologically, that of: **objectivity and subjectivity**.

Except, it is not a terrain, nor is it a boxing match—but *freudian in slip*: objectivity and subjectivity have long been treated as a married couple that refuses divorce, and we, their children, have suffered the consequences. 

Though, such [*fabled tellings*, *tell*](../bat[hh]ouse/2025_07_22_o_my_fable.md), a story of **siblings mistaken and taken spousal**: a tragic romance, rosed to greek.

We may re-enter the *‘now’*: hat in hand, fable aligned and canon situated. And, as we, with *much too seem*, entered the taboo, let us be radical—not by way of Sigmoid, but **paradox**—and propose that, perhaps:

> **Objectivity is not simply void of bias, but the accumulation, of said bias.**

A moment may be taken, for such a statement is greek in itself, is it not? Though, some semblance can be found if we truly look to ourselves and understand what we really are, and this is: *we are narrators, and **unreliable** ones at that.*

We narrate unreliably, insofar that we **misappropriate our own biases as truth, which we then marry into objectivity**. Yet, the danger is not that of unreliability, nor is it in the biased truths we tell. Such danger is housed by the perpetual refusal to outgrow the biases we hold. For, the hat may swap hands, but if biases are left to bloom in the absence of scrutiny, we are left with *truth unturned and algaised*: **knowledge systems eutrophed, and eutrophying.**

In such eutrophication we may lament, and so wonder, ‘*where is the sheep?*’ And, much like most families, the dynamic here is not too unfamiliar. 

In that, **subjectivity is presupposed maudlin, and such a condition is far too asymmetric**, and so the noun failed to morph by camel, and landed by sheep. As we are, in the *‘now’* and permitted to lament and wonder, one may consider comfort, and lull, and vision haze of sheep, and further in such consideration may **lament** into:

> **Subjectivity not as the gateway to bias, but that which modulates bias accumulated through one’s emotional well-being.**

And so, under such greek musing, perhaps we should consider such sibling relation with gravity rather than a dismissal to marriage. 

If, we are to ever sit with truth, **we must not seek absolute certainty, but to tread towards momentary coherence**: *an alignment that brings satisfaction to *‘now’*, but remains open to evolution*.
