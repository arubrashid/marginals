># cerebral
>
>If words are **oil**, then:
>>*Christina Rossetti’s ‘[Who Has Seen the Wind?](./2025_07_26_kun_fa_yakun.md)’* 
>
>is **slick** in emuls refuse, 
>and it is from within such refusal I **write**:   
>
>>with words **in** stain;    
>>elastic and treble,    
>>and carp.    
>
>For, I will pass, and oil will lift: *cerebral in fact*.    
>
>The **wind** will not be seen; for, it will simply, just, **be**.
