# cerebral



If words are **oil**, then Christina Rossetti’s ‘[Who Has Seen the Wind?](./2025_07_26_kun_fa_yakun.md)’ is **slick** in emuls refuse, and it is from within such refusal I **write**:   

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;with words **in** stain;    
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;elastic and treble,    
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;and carp.    

For, I will pass, and oil will lift: `cerebral in fact`.    

The **wind** will not be seen; for, it will simply, just, **be**.
